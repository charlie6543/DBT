About

This website is still in the early stages of development.

The developer is a college student currently going through the DBT program. This website was made to learn HTML, review the lessons, be a resource for them to fill out diary cards, and provide an accessible resource for those who are interested in DBT. The developer does NOT have any psychiatric, counselling, or healthcare training or experience. This website is not an effective replacement for therapy or counselling.

This website builds strictly from DBT worksheets, specifically from DBT Skills Training Handouts and Worksheets, Second Edition by Marsha M. Linehan, 2015.
